Bio

I am a research assistant at the Pervasive AI Research (PAIR) Labs developing an autonomous intelligent control system for drones. My research focuses on UAV computer vision including 3D localization, image restoration, light-weight processing techniques. To solve real-world problems under uncertainty, I aims to incorporate conventional computer vision method and DL-based approaches, and also take advantage of geometric and temperoal information to make the estimation more robust.
